Create Your Own Pizza
Inspired by the Create Your Own Pizza re: every single online 
pizza ordering shop, you can create and print a receipt for 
your very own custom made pizza! Add red or white sauce! 
Choose pepperoni, sausage, or canadian bacon. Add vegetables! Or
choose from any of the four premade pizzas. Don't forget to
save! Once you do, you'll be able to print your receipt so you
remember what you made last time.
